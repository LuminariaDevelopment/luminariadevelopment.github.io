Level Design

Level design in video games is making engaging gameplay design that shape the player's experience by carefully arranging different elements. It doesn't sound very hard, but in reality, it is. Level design is also an important part of game development, as it plays role in delivering a memorable experience to players.

Enviornmental Design

Environment Design involves creating the visual and atmospheric aspects of a game world. This includes designing the landscape, terrain, structures, and overall ambiance of the game environment, with a focus on aesthetics and immersion.
