Importance of Environmental Design

So, you're making a game and your done with the core mechanics and stuff, but your game level doesn't look so good, that's where I come in, the importance of level design in games cannot be overlooked. It serves as the foundation which a player's entire experience is built. A well-made level can keep players engaged, making it a vital component in creating enjoyable gaming experiences.

What I Have Made

Over the past years, I have made loads of levels for game jams, personal projects, and for others. From peaceful forests to chaotic cities, from apocalyptic cities to cosmic realms. Each new project has been an opportunity for me to explore unique themes, challenge my design skills, and bring immersive experiences to players.
