Fragile Little Boat

A fun little delivery game.

Game Description

Play as a small boat and your mission is to deliver as much things as possible, but be careful, because if you hit something or get hit by a cannonball, your boat will explode, use your map to figure out where you are, and beside the map there will be a list showing the thing you need to deliver, your total score, and how much time you have left to deliver. Go from port to port and stay inside the time limit or else your score will go down!

Game Information

Since this is published with WebGL 1 API, it uses gamma color space, this is why it looks a bit off.
